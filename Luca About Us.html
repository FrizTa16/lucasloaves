About LUCA'S LOAVES

Luca's Loaves is an artisan sourdough bread bakery located in Paramatta. Founded by Luca, who discovered his passion for breadmaking after being laid off from his job as a lifeguard, the bakery has grown from a small home-based business to a thriving local bakery. At Luca's Loaves, we believe in using only the finest organic ingredients, prepared with care and dedication. Our bread is hand-kneaded and shaped in-house, with no store-bought yeast, and baked to perfection in our solar-powered oven. We are committed to sustainability and use only filtered water in our baking process. Our bread is easy to digest and a great alternative to store-bought bread, which is often mixed and baked within 2 hours, resulting in a high gluten content. Our sourdough bread is prepared over 14-17 hours, allowing the gluten to break down, making it easier to digest.
